Environment “mapping”
We will form a barricaded square region ±120 cm × 120 cm. Your robot will be placed within this
space and given 5 minutes to move about. The environment will contain exactly one obstacle,
which will be either a circular disk or a triangle. The robot must be placed to start within a corner.
At the end of the 5 minutes, your robot should report which of the two cases applies to that
environment.
To complete this:
1. Your robot should operate autonomously.
2. Your robot may modify the environment in any non-destructive fashion†; no person in
your team is permitted to modify or affix items to the interior of the environment.
3. The robot can report the final classification via LEDs, sounds, wifi, etc.
Note: the shapes will be available for your examination during the development phase.
† if you need clarification on what constitutes destruction, please ask the instructors.
You will be scored by:
(i) an evaluation of a randomly selected environment type;
(ii) a run commences ends after five minutes;
(iii) you get performance points as follows: 5 points awarded if, at termination, the robot has
successfully identified the environment type. Additional bonus points may be awarded for
elegant software or hardware designs.
